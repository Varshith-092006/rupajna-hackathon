About RūpaJña

Turning Innovation Into Impactful Reality

RūpaJña is a national level prototype challenge that brings together innovators, developers, and problem-solvers to address pressing challenges in various domains. Our mission is to foster creativity and collaboration while developing practical solutions that can make a real impact.

This year's event focuses on three key thrust areas: IoT, Biomedical, and AgriTech. Participants will have the opportunity to work on problem statements provided by industry experts and develop innovative prototypes.
